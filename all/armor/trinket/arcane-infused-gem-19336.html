Arcane Infused Gem
Binds when picked up
Unique
Trinket
Classes: Hunter
Requires Level 60
Use: Infuses you with Arcane energy, causing your next Arcane Shot fired within 10 seconds to detonate at the target. The Arcane Detonation will deal 185 to 216 damage to enemies near the target.
Added in patch 1.6
